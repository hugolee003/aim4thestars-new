Robotics

Course Overview

About the Course

This course is designed for beginners who want to learn the fundamentals of robotics. From basic electronics and sensors to building and programming robots, this course will provide you with a solid foundation in robotics and automation. You will learn how to work with components like motors and microcontrollers, write simple code to control robot actions, and understand how mechanical and electronic systems work together. The course is structured to help you build your skills step by step, with plenty of hands-on projects and challenges to reinforce your learning.

In this course you will be able to

  • Understand the fundamental concepts of robotics, including sensors, motors, and microcontrollers
    • Learn how to build and program simple robots using beginner-friendly platforms like Arduino
    • Explore the basics of electronics, coding, and mechanical systems
    • Develop problem-solving skills through hands-on robotics projects and challenges
  • Upon successful completion of the program, participants will be proudly awarded a Certificate of Completion to recognize their achievement.
